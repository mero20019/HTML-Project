Yi Jinyu, a staff member of the Yiyang residential complex in Yichun City, Jiangxi Province, operates a drone to measure temperature and record it remotely to residents, to avoid the occurrence of a second infection caused by close contact. Yi Jinyu said that a drone equipped with an infrared thermal imaging lens can monitor visible light and infrared light, and can measure the temperature within a range of 3 meters with a 5% error rate, and the error rate has decreased to 1% within 1 meter.
